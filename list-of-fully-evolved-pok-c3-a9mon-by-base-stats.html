List of fully evolved Pokmon by base stats

From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia.

Base stats are an important defining characteristic of each Pokémon species. Below appears a list of all Pokémon that are either fully evolved or do not evolve by their base stats. The Mega Evolutions and the different Formes of these Pokémon are also given their own entries, and the list is able to be changed at will to list them in order of any one of the six stat categories, as well as the total of all of their base stats, the average of all of their base stats, or they can simply be listed either alphabetically or by their National Pokédex number.
