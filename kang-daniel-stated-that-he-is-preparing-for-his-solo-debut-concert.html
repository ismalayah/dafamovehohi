Kang Daniel Stated That He Is Preparing For His Solo Debut Concert

DANITYs, mark your calendars. Kang Daniel is planning a new concert. On July 4, the exceptional soloist said that he is now preparing for his first solo show, named First Parade in Seoul, which will take place at Seoul’s Jangchung Arena on August 13 and 14. The August 13 event is scheduled for 6 p.m. KST (2:30 p.m. IST) on Saturday, while the August 14 concert is scheduled for 5 p.m. KST on Sunday (1:30 pm IST).

Not only that but the show will be streamed life online for his global audience to enjoy. In the following days, tickets will be available for purchase. The presale for the Seoul performances will begin on July 8 and the regular sale will begin on July 15. Kang Daniel will perform his first solo show since his debut in July 2019 with the EP Color On Me, which was published on July 25, 2019. The concert date also coincides with the third anniversary of Kang Daniel’s initial announcement of his official fan club’s name to be ‘DANITY,’ on August 29, 2019, making the forthcoming concerts even more significant.

Kang Daniel jokes that he misses his followers as he prepares to announce his first solo performance.

The term “Initial” in the concert title refers to a festival commemorating the first beginning. Kang Daniel’s popular B-Side tune off his newly released full-length album, The Story, is called “Parade.” Seoul, of course, is his own country’s capital and the starting point for his voyage.

Kang Daniel expressed his delight in a statement:

“I’ve missed you terribly, and I’m delighted that we can finally be together via this performance.” I’m even more eager since this is my first solo performance. I’m going to emerge on stage determined to collapse [to go all out], so the audience should likewise prepare carefully.”

The Parade singer hinted at future performances and abroad tours in a recent interview with Forbes magazine. After finishing his Seoul tour, he disclosed his aspirations for a globe tour. He said that he is preparing for the Summer Sonic concert in Japan, among other things. He also expressed his earnest wish to meet DANITYs throughout his worldwide promotional travels and encouraged them to stay tuned for future information.

Kang Daniel will play at Summer Sonic in August in Japan.

As previously stated, the outstanding soloist and entrepreneur will play at Summer Sonic 2022, Japan’s biggest music event, in August. This was made clear by his new partnership with Warner Music Japan.

Summer Sonic, one of Japan’s largest summer outdoor music events, will be held concurrently in Tokyo and Osaka from August 20 to 21 this year. TOMORROW X TOGETHER, CL, Zico, and Seori will also play in Tokyo on August 20 and Osaka on August 21. On August 21, the Upside Down vocalist will take the Pacific stage. Summer Sonic 2022 will also include international performers such as 1975, Post Malone, ONE OK ROCK, Megan Thee Stallion, Rina Sawayama, and others.
