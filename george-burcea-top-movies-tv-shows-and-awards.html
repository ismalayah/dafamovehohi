George Burcea top movies, TV shows and awards

George Burcea is a Romanian film and theater actor. He was born on January 15, 1988 in Bucharest. Before studying acting, he was a sailor. George Burcea played Lurch in Tv series “Wednesday” by Tim Burton.

The 34-year-old actor has had a stunning playing career and has become well-known and well-liked for his important roles in numerous movies and TV shows.

The main trailer for Wednesday, Tim Burton’s adaptation of the Addams Family series, in which he plays Lurch, was recently released on Netflix.

George Burcea  top movies and TV shows

The Romanian entertainer made his film debut in Thailand’s “O Luna” in 2012. His other notable performances include his roles in the films “Say Yes”, “Prietena mea e mafiota (2015)”, and the television series “Comrade Detective (2018)” and “Wednesday.”

George Burcea awards and nominations

Burcea has featured in quite a number of movies since his debut but he is yet to bag some awards.
