The Rock Is Proud Of Simone, Says She Kicks Ass In The Ring

The Rock was recently interviewed by Access. During it, Dwayne Johnson opened up about his daughter Simone joining WWE. He’s proud of her for entering a “difficult world – not only to be in, but to make it in,” and said that it’s “doubly difficult” when you’re a woman.

“She goes in there and not only does she have big shoes to fill and is well aware of our family name, but also has this desire to make her own path,” The Rock said. “Which is the best part about it.”

When asked if he’s seen Simone wrestle, Rock said that she’s “badass” and that she “kicks ass” inside the ring.
