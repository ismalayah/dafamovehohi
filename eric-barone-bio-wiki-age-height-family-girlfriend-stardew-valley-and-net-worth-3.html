    Eric Barone Career

    Barone began working on Stardew Valley in 2012 and released it in 2016. He was praised for creating the game entirely independently.

    He did everything from making the characters, writing the dialogue, creating the music, and animating every pixel. Barone currently develops the game with one other person.

    Barone announced that he is working on several new games with at least one of them set to be in the world of Stardew Valley. Another game that he is working on is also tied with Stardew Valley in some way.

    In February 2021, Barone and board game designer Cole Meideros released a cooperative board game adaptation of Stardew Valley titled Stardew Valley: The Board Game.
