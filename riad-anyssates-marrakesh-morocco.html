Riad Anyssates, Marrakesh, Morocco

​Nestled into Marrakesh’s medina, lies our guesthouse.
Riad Anyssates benefits from an ideal location in the Red city: between the souks next to the famous Jemaa El Fna square, the Koutoubia and Majorelle Gardens in the new town (Gueliz).
The many palaces, gardens, museums and the remparts are enough to quell any thirst for oriental culture.
Morocco is a popular golfing destination and we can help you organize your stay golfRiad Anyssates will be your oasis, the place you return to after a busy day of exploration, for enjoying a traditional mint tea with Moroccan pastries in the riad’s relaxing atmosphere.
