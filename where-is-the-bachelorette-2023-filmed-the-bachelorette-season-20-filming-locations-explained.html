With The Bachelorette season 20 upon us, fans have started expressing their curiosities regarding the show’s various aspects. One of which is which one among the suitable gentlemen will finally win the heart of our new Bachelorette, Charity Lawson.

Added to this is an interest in learning about the various filming locations – which if you think about it, makes sense given the show’s history of presenting some of the more exotic locations and luxury resorts to its viewers. A major part of the filming was done in California with various other areas within the United States serving as the harbor for memorable dating episodes and moments.

Where was this season filmed?

Some of the major locations include Agoura Hills in Los Angeles County where the iconic Bachelor mansion is located. Agoura Hills is situated in the Santa Monica region of the state, which is also the area where a majority of the filming was conducted. Other places include Hollywood and San Diego County within the state.

Hollywood — especially the popular Hollywood sign — was the chosen area for Charity to get well-acquainted with her potential partners when she went on a group date with them. And Petco Park in San Diego is where she went on a date with Brayden Bowers.

Apart from California, the sparsely-populated city of Louisiana – New Orleans served as a major filming location. Several scenes were shot in the city and as per many sources, Chase was occasionally found filming for the show in the region. Of all the areas, Jackson Square was a significant site where Chase was seen going on a date with Joey Graziadei.

The season finale will indeed be an aesthetic treat for the audience as most of the filming took place in some of the picturesque islands of Fiji well known for their immensely beautiful landscape and white beaches.

Given the breathtakingly captivating areas which will undoubtedly add flavor to the show, it will definitely be worth multiple viewings.

The Bachelorette will premiere on June 26, 2023.
