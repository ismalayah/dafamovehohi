Oliver Heldens Drops HI-LO Collab With Chocolate Puma

Following up on the release of his latest single under his given name, Oliver Heldens is reigniting his HI-LO side moniker as he offers up his new collaboration with Chocolate Puma. Heldens had first teased the new single earlier in the month when he dropped a preview of “Steam Train,” and now the full collaboration is available for streaming.

Recommended Videos

On “Steam Train,” Oliver Heldens and Chocolate Puma deliver a churning blend of tech-house rhythms and minimalistic melody perfect for the dancefloors. After a lengthy percussion driven kick-off, the new tune introduces a repeated flute phrase that injects an ominous vibe into the mix before the same melody is transposed onto sinister rave synths during a breakdown midway through. “Steam Train” offers up the usual underground dance vibes present on HI-LO releases, allowing each element to shine throughout the streamlined arrangement.

The latest HI-LO effort ranks high with its dance-dedicated grooves and stark production values, proving that the pair up with Chocolate Puma is a worthy collaboration.
